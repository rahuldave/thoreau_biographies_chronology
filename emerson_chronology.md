# Ralph Waldo Emerson Chronology

Source keys are defined in `the_book_that_changed_america_chronology.md`.
Emerson was not one of the four people at Sanborn's January 1, 1860 dinner, but
Fuller's Concord story repeatedly depends on Emerson's house, library, family,
Saturday Club circle, and influence on Thoreau, Sanborn, and Alcott.

## Arc in Fuller

Emerson is the gravitational center of Concord rather than the most direct
Darwinian reader. Fuller uses him to stage the tension between Transcendental
idealism and Darwinian naturalism: Emerson is curious and receptive, but it is
Thoreau who turns the shock of Origin into sustained ecological practice.
