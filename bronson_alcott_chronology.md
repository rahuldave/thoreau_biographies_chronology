## Arc in Fuller

Alcott receives Darwin as a spiritual and philosophical challenge rather than a
scientific program. Fuller uses him as a foil to Thoreau: both are in the same
Concord rooms, but Thoreau turns Origin into observation and ecological method,
while Alcott tries to absorb it into a preexisting idealist cosmology.
